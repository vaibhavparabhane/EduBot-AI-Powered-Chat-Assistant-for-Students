What is the STAR method in interviews?
The STAR method stands for Situation, Task, Action, and Result. It's used to structure responses to behavioral questions effectively.

Tell me about yourself.
Start with your current role or education, highlight key achievements or internships, and conclude with your career goals.

How should I answer 'Why should we hire you?' 
Focus on your skills, past achievements, and how you align with the company’s needs. Be confident and concise.

What is your greatest strength?
Choose a strength that aligns with the role, and back it up with an example—like adaptability, communication, or problem-solving.

What is your biggest weakness?
Choose a real but non-critical weakness, and explain how you're actively working to improve it.

How do I prepare for technical interviews?
Practice DSA, review your projects, understand system design basics, and brush up on language-specific fundamentals.

What is object-oriented programming?
OOP is a programming paradigm based on objects containing data and methods. Core concepts include encapsulation, inheritance, and polymorphism.

What is a data structure?
Data structures are ways to store and organize data efficiently. Examples include arrays, linked lists, stacks, and trees.

What is Big O notation?
Big O describes the time or space complexity of an algorithm in terms of input size, helping evaluate performance.

Explain a project you worked on.
Describe the problem, the tech stack used, your role, challenges faced, and the final outcome or results.

What is the difference between Array and Linked List?
Arrays have fixed size and allow random access, while linked lists are dynamic and efficient at insertions/deletions.

What is normalization in databases?
Normalization is the process of organizing data to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity in relational databases.

What is the difference between primary key and foreign key?
A primary key uniquely identifies records in a table; a foreign key links records between two tables.

What is SQL JOIN?
A JOIN is used to combine rows from two or more tables based on a related column.

What is the difference between REST and SOAP APIs?
REST is stateless, simpler, and uses JSON, while SOAP is protocol-based, more secure, and uses XML.

How do I handle rejection in interviews?
Stay positive, ask for feedback, improve your weak areas, and treat every interview as a learning experience.

What questions can I ask the interviewer?
Ask about team structure, technologies used, growth opportunities, and expectations for the role.

What is your salary expectation?
Research market rates and give a reasonable range, or say you're open to discussing a fair offer based on the role.

How do I make my resume stand out?
Focus on measurable achievements, use action verbs, tailor it to the job, and keep it clean and readable.

Should I add a photo to my resume?
It's not required unless specifically asked. Prioritize content, clarity, and relevance.

What are soft skills and why are they important?
Soft skills include communication, adaptability, teamwork, and time management. They complement technical skills in most jobs.

How do I negotiate my first job offer?
Express gratitude, understand the offer, and if necessary, respectfully ask for a better package based on market research.

Can you help me prepare for HR interviews?
Yes! Practice common behavioral questions, know your resume well, and be ready to talk about your values, goals, and personality.

How do I explain a gap in my education or work?
Be honest. Focus on what you learned or did during that period, such as courses, projects, or personal growth.

What is group discussion and how do I ace it?
Group discussion evaluates communication and thinking skills. Stay calm, make logical points, and listen actively to others.

Tell me about yourself.
Give a short, 1-minute summary of your key skills and why you are interested in this role.

What are your strengths?
Name one or two key strengths from the job description and give a real example of when you used them.

What are your weaknesses?
Mention a real weakness you are actively improving. This shows that you are self-aware.

What do you know about our company?
Mention their products, mission, or recent news. Show that you have done your homework and are genuinely interested.

Why do you want to work here?
Talk about the company's values, projects, or culture that excite you and align with your own goals.

Why should we hire you?
Explain how your specific skills and experience are a perfect match for the job requirements listed.

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Describe your ambition to grow your skills and take on more responsibility, ideally at this company.

How do you handle stress or pressure?
Mention staying calm, focusing on a plan, prioritizing tasks, and not being afraid to ask for help when needed.

Describe a difficult problem you solved.
Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to explain a challenging situation and how you overcame it.

Tell me about a time you worked in a team.
Describe your role in the team and how you contributed to a successful outcome. Focus on collaboration and communication.

How do you handle feedback or criticism?
Explain that you see feedback as a valuable opportunity to learn and grow, not as a personal attack.

What motivates you?
Talk about what drives you, such as solving challenging problems, learning new technologies, or contributing to a team goal.

Tell me about a project you are proud of.
Briefly describe a project. Explain the goal, what you did, the technologies you used, and what the final result was.

How do you stay updated with technology?
Mention reading tech blogs, following experts on social media, taking online courses, or working on personal projects.

Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly.
Describe the situation, what you needed to learn, and the steps you took to learn it efficiently.

What is Python?
Python is a popular, high-level programming language known for its simple and readable code.

What is a variable in programming?
A variable is a name that refers to a value. It's a container for storing data.

What is a function?
A function is a reusable block of code that performs a specific action when called.

What is a loop?
A loop is a control structure used to repeatedly execute a block of code as long as a condition is true.

What is an algorithm?
An algorithm is a set of step-by-step instructions designed to perform a specific task or solve a problem.

What is a data structure?
A data structure is a specialized format for organizing, processing, retrieving and storing data.

What is an API?
An API (Application Programming Interface) is a set of rules that allows different software applications to communicate with each other.

What is SQL?
SQL (Structured Query Language) is the standard language for managing and communicating with relational databases.

What is a database table?
A table is a collection of related data held in a structured format within a database, consisting of columns and rows.

What is a primary key?
A primary key is a unique identifier for each record in a database table. It cannot have NULL values.

What is Git?
Git is a distributed version control system used to track changes in source code during software development.

What is a 'commit' in Git?
A 'commit' is like a snapshot of your files at a specific point in time. It saves your changes to the local repository.

What is HTML?
HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the standard language for creating the structure of web pages.

What is CSS?
CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is a language used to describe the presentation and styling of a web page written in HTML.

What is your preferred work environment?
Describe an environment where you are most productive, e.g., collaborative, independent, fast-paced, or structured.

How do you organize your work?
Mention techniques like using to-do lists, project management tools, or prioritizing tasks based on urgency and importance.

What are your hobbies or interests outside of work?
Share a genuine hobby. This helps the interviewer see you as a well-rounded person.

What are your salary expectations?
Research the market rate for the role in your location and provide a reasonable salary range, showing flexibility.

When can you start?
Be realistic. If you are currently employed, mention your required notice period. Otherwise, state your availability.

Do you have any questions for us?
Yes! Always have at least two thoughtful questions ready to ask about the team, the role, or the company culture.

Tell me about a time you failed.
Describe a situation where things didn't go as planned, what you learned, and how you applied that lesson later.
